### What didn't

- **Hero inline email capture: 0 submissions in 7 days.** 0/21 with the popup on the same page getting 2/17. Either the form is too quiet ("Or grab the free PDF" divider above a single email field below a louder primary button), or visitors who want the PDF take the popup path. This is the clearest signal — but the sample is still small.
- **Footer CTA bar: 0 clicks.** Only ~3 visitors hit 100% scroll on homepage, so this might just be sample size. Re-judge next week.
